Let’s Talk Genomics

A toolkit for citizen engagement

Genomics has the potential to transform healthcare and society. By preventing illness, improving diagnosis and tailoring individual treatments, it can contribute to better health for everyone. As technologies develop rapidly, they also raise important ethical and societal questions. It is therefore essential to engage the public in open discussions about the possibilities, limitations and broader impacts to ensure that the development and use of genomics reflect shared values and concerns.

The website *Let’s Talk Genomics* presents key ethical and societal topics in genomics and provides tools to support meaningful conversations with citizens. By encouraging informed dialogue and reflection, this initiative aims to help shape responsible and socially supported use of genomics in society.
