Connecting genomics across Europe

A shared approach for secure, ethical and interoperable genomic data sharing across Europe

Explore the Framework About the Framework

The 1+ Million Genomes (1+MG) Framework provides guidelines for the implementation of the 1+MG initiative, which is enabling secure access to genomics and the corresponding clinical data across Europe for better research, personalised healthcare and health policy making.

Projects

The 1+MG Framework is built from the outputs of the 1+MG project family, including B1MG, B1MG+, GDI (Genomic Data Infrastructure) and Genome of Europe. These projects have developed the foundational components, infrastructure and policy coordination needed to realise the 1+MG Framework.
